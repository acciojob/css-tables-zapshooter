## Styling information

1. `td` and `th` will have a `border` of `1px solid #ccc` and `border-radius` of `5px`. They should also have a `padding` of `0.5rem`.
2. Background color of `odd` rows should be `#f2f2f2` and of `even` rows should be `#ffffff`
